How do I create my own IPTV playlist?

Use a text editor to create an M3U file with format: #EXTM3U then #EXTINF:-1 tvg-name="Channel Name" tvg-logo="URL" group-title="Category",http://stream-url. Save as playlist.m3u and upload to your IPTV app.
