How do I fix IPTV freezing?

Freezing is often caused by unstable connections or server issues. Try: switching to a wired connection, lowering video quality, using a VPN, clearing app cache, updating the app, or changing DNS to Cloudflare (1.1.1.1) or Google (8.8.8.8).
